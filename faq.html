Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

For Students
Q1: What is this portal used for?

A: This portal is your one-stop place to get technical and administrative support. You can submit tickets for issues related to departments and other IT or administrative queries.

Q2: How do I create a new support ticket?

A: After logging into your student dashboard, click the "Create a Ticket" button. Fill in the required fields, select the relevant department and click the create button. You can view your created ticket by clicking "View all Tickets" on the student dashboard.

Q3: How can I check the status of my submitted tickets?

A: You can check your ticket status by clicking 'View all Tickets' on the student dashboard. Refer to the status column to see whether a staff member has replied to your question.
